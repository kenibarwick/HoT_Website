FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Bedford Techno Independence Day

What's included with my ticket?

Your ticket includes entry to the event from 6PM, access to the main marquee, full DJ lineup performances, and eligibility for the sports bra special offer (free shot at the bar if wearing a sports bra).

Can I get a refund on my ticket?

Generally, no refunds are available for purchased tickets. However, if the event is cancelled (not postponed), full refunds will be issued. For exceptional circumstances, please contact us directly.

What's the sports bra offer?

Anyone wearing a sports bra to the event gets a FREE SHOT at the bar! This unique offer celebrates Bedford's sporting spirit and connects to DJ Sportsbra's theme. One free shot per person, must be wearing sports bra upon entry.

Are there group discounts available?

Yes! Groups of 6-10 get 10% discount, groups of 11+ get 15% discount plus a reserved area. Birthday groups get an additional free shot for the birthday person. Contact us for group bookings.

Where exactly is The Balloon Pub?

The Balloon Pub is located on High Street in Bedford town center (postcode MK40 1RU). It's about 10 minutes walk from Bedford railway station and easily accessible by car, bus, or taxi.

Is there parking available?

Limited on-street parking is available. We recommend using nearby car parks: Allhallows Car Park (5 min walk), Lurke Street Car Park (3 min walk), or Dame Alice Street Car Park (7 min walk). Evening rates typically apply after 6PM.

What happens if it rains?

No worries! We have a massive marquee setup that provides full weather protection. Rain or shine, the party continues with professional sound and lighting systems under cover.

What time does the event start and finish?

Doors open at 6PM. keni.dj starts at 7PM, DJ Sportsbra at 9PM, with a B2B session from 11PM. The event runs late into the night, typically finishing around 2AM.

What's the age restriction?

This is an 18+ event only. Valid photo ID is required for entry (driving license, passport, or official ID card). No exceptions will be made.

What's the dress code?

Come as you are! There's no strict dress code, but we encourage you to express yourself. Remember, wearing a sports bra gets you a free shot at the bar! Comfortable dancing shoes recommended.

Can I bring my own drinks?

No outside food or drinks are permitted. The venue has a full bar with competitive prices serving beers, spirits, cocktails, and soft drinks throughout the event.

What security measures are in place?

Professional security personnel will be on-site throughout the event. The venue has comprehensive CCTV coverage, and bag searches may be conducted at entry. First aid facilities are available.

Are there any prohibited items?

Prohibited items include: outside food/drinks, professional cameras, illegal substances, weapons of any kind, glass bottles, and anything deemed inappropriate by security. Phone cameras are fine for personal use.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, The Balloon Pub has wheelchair accessible entrance and facilities. If you have specific accessibility requirements, please contact us in advance so we can ensure the best possible experience.

Can I re-enter if I leave the venue?

No, there is no re-entry policy. Once you leave the venue, you cannot return. Please plan accordingly and make sure you have everything you need before entering.

Will there be food available?

The venue focuses on drinks rather than food service during the event. However, there are numerous restaurants, takeaways, and late-night food options within walking distance on Bedford High Street.

I lost my ticket confirmation - what should I do?

Contact us immediately with your booking details. We can resend confirmation emails or help locate your booking. Have your name, email address, and approximate purchase date ready.
