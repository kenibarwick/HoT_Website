Yes! This is a completely FREE event - no tickets, no cover charge, no hidden costs. Just turn up at The Balloon Pub from 6PM on July 4th and join the rebellion! üéâ

What time does the event start and finish? +

Doors open at 6PM and the event runs until late. Our DJs will be bringing the beats throughout the evening, with the venue staying open for after-party vibes!

What's this sports bra offer about? +

Anyone bold enough to sport a sports bra gets a FREE SHOT at the bar! It's our tribute to Bedford's sporting heritage and DJ Sportsbra. Whether you're supporting Bedford Blues, Real Bedford FC Pirates, or just feeling the sporting spirit - sport the bra, get the shot! üèÉ‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏èü•É

No! The Balloon Pub no on-site parking available, but there is nearby street parking. It's also well-connected to Bedford town centre and train station via public transport. Plan your journey and consider carpooling with fellow rebels!

Nope! Just turn up on the night. However, capacity is limited so we recommend arriving early (doors open at 6PM) to guarantee entry. First come, first served for this epic free event!
