‚ùì FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What time does the event start and finish? +

Doors open at 6PM and the event runs until late. DJ Sportsbra kicks off from 6PM-8:30PM, followed by keni.dj from 8:30PM-11PM. The venue may stay open later for after-party vibes!

What's this sports bra offer about? +

Anyone bold enough to sport a sports bra gets a FREE SHOT at the bar! It's our tribute to Bedford's sporting heritage and DJ Sportsbra. Whether you're supporting Bedford Blues, Real Bedford FC Pirates, or just feeling the sporting spirit - sport the bra, get the shot! üèÉ‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏èü•É

Is there parking at the venue? +

Yes! The Balloon Pub has on-site parking available, plus nearby street parking. We're also well-connected to Bedford town centre and train station via public transport. Plan your journey and consider carpooling with fellow rebels!

What's the age limit and dress code? +

This is an 18+ event and valid ID is required for entry. Dress code is casual/rave wear - come comfortable and ready to dance! Sports bras are actively encouraged for our special drink offer. No offensive clothing or items that could be used as weapons.

Can I buy tickets on the door? +

Door tickets may be available for ¬£20 (cash only) but are subject to capacity and not guaranteed. We strongly recommend booking online in advance to avoid disappointment - this rebellion is going to be massive!

What if I lose my ticket or can't attend? +

If you lose your ticket, contact us with your purchase details and we'll sort you out. Tickets can be transferred to friends via email. Unfortunately, we can't offer refunds unless the event is cancelled, but you can always pass your ticket to a fellow Bedford rebel!

Is Bedford really not shit? +

Absolutely not! This HoT event is proof that Bedford has a thriving underground scene. While other towns are dead boring, we're bringing the heat that puts Bedford on the bloody map. Come join us and help shut up anyone who says Bedford's rubbish! üí™üî•
