The Balloon Pub - Bedford's perfect setting for electronic revolution

THE BALLOON PUB

High Street, Bedford

Bedfordshire, MK40 1RU

The Balloon Pub stands as one of Bedford's most beloved venues, perfectly positioned in the heart of the town center. With its rich history and vibrant atmosphere, it's the ideal location for Bedford Techno Independence Day.

For this special event, we're transforming the space with a Epic marquee setup that will house our state-of-the-art sound system and lighting rig. The indoor-outdoor flow creates the perfect festival atmosphere while maintaining the intimate feel that makes Bedford's music scene so special.

Why The Balloon Pub?

  • Professional sound system installation
  • Epic marquee for weather protection
  • Central Bedford location
  • Full bar service throughout event
  • Accessible facilities
  • Professional security team

LOCATION & DIRECTIONS

Getting There

By Car

The Balloon Pub is located on Bedford High Street, easily accessible from the A6 and A421. Use postcode MK40 1RU for sat nav.

Parking: Limited on-street parking. We recommend using nearby car parks:

  • Allhallows Car Park (5 min walk)
  • Lurke Street Car Park (3 min walk)
  • Dame Alice Street Car Park (7 min walk)

By Train

Bedford Railway Station is approximately 10 minutes walk from The Balloon Pub. Regular services from London St Pancras (50 minutes).

Last trains: Check National Rail for return journey times, especially for London services.

By Bus

Multiple bus routes serve Bedford town center. The venue is a short walk from the main bus stops on Silver Street and Midland Road.

Taxi / Uber

Taxis readily available throughout Bedford. The venue is well-known to local drivers. Book in advance for your return journey!
