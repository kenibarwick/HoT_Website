FEATURED ARTISTS

keni.dj

Keni Barwick

Underground Techno | House | Electronic

Keni Barwick, performing as keni.dj, is one of Bedford's most innovative electronic music artists. Known for seamlessly blending underground techno with progressive house elements, Keni has been at the forefront of the local electronic scene for several years.

With a deep understanding of dancefloor dynamics and an ear for cutting-edge sounds, keni.dj creates sets that take listeners on a journey from deep, hypnotic grooves to peak-time bangers. Their technical prowess and track selection have made them a standout performer in Bedford's underground music community.

For Bedford Techno Independence Day, expect a masterclass in electronic music curation that showcases why Bedford's underground scene is second to none.

DJ Sportsbra

Mark Bowler

Banging Techno | Electronic | Progressive

Mark Bowler, known in the scene as DJ Sportsbra, brings an explosive energy to every performance that perfectly embodies Bedford's rebellious spirit. The unique moniker isn't just a name - it's a statement about breaking boundaries and challenging expectations in electronic music.

Specializing in high-energy techno and progressive electronic sounds, DJ Sportsbra has built a reputation for sets that hit hard and keep the dancefloor moving. Their ability to read a crowd and deliver exactly what's needed at the perfect moment has made them a favorite at local events.

With a playful yet professional approach to DJing, Mark brings both technical skill and personality to every performance. Expect big drops, crowd interaction, and the kind of energy that makes Bedford's nightlife legendary.
